Problem Statement & Analysis

  • Lack of Transparency:

    Consumers lack vital information → Erosion of Trust → Sourcing uncertainty creates doubts → Consumer Confidence Drops.

  • Inefficient Traceability:

    Limited Visibility → Delayed Response → Ineffective Mitigation.

  • Proliferation of Counterfeit Products:

    Weak checks in place → Exploitation of supply chain weaknesses by counterfeiters → Flood of stores with fake mangoes.
